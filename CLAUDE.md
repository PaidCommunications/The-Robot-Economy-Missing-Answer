## Working Conventions

- The numbered markdown files (`01-` through `10-` plus `appendix-notes.md`) are the canonical draft.
- `The-Robot-Economys-Missing-Answer.md` is the archived single-file version (retained for reference).
- `article rough` is the original unformatted draft (retained for reference).
- Session notes go in `session-notes/` with date-prefixed filenames.
- Factual claims should be sourced. The essay includes endnotes [^1]–[^30] in `appendix-notes.md`.
- When updating statistics or claims, note the source and date of the data.
